Game Rules

  • Stone beats scissors,scissors beat paper,and paper beats stone.
  • Agree ahead of time whether you'll count off “stone,paper,scissors,shoot” or just stone,paper,scissors.”
  • Use stone,paper,scissors to settle minor decisions or simply play to pass the time
  • If both players lay down the same hand,each player lays down another hand
